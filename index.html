Important notes from last meeting

  • All participants are assumed at their power from the end of the series.
  • Particopants will not be allowed shikigami or outside help unless the help is specific to them (aka Rika for My JJK OC).
  • Matches will be assumed in a generic main city like Shibuya with no other forms of life.
  • Fleeing for an extended period of time is considered a forfeit.

Speculative Match Reults

For each individual match, a short discussion will be held to decide who is more likely to win and what methods each participant has to win their match.

Match 1: Blue Judas vs Kaisen

Predicted Winner: Blue Judas

The reason we believe Blue Judas would win is because of his unrivaled combat capabilities. With no apparent weaknesses and unmatched feats it is near impossible to imagine Blue Judas losing a fight.

Match 2: Red Judas vs Bubble Pop Electric

Predicted Winner: Red Judas

Another contender in a league of his own, despite Bubble Pop Electric's ability to move at supersonic speeds, Red Judas's immense power and destructive capabilities simply have no opening despite the speed of his opponent.

Match 3: My JJK OC vs Tuka Donka

Predicted Winner: Undecided

This fight we find ultimately undecided due to the unique combat abilities of Tuka Donka. While My JJK OC has a plethora of speical abilities through his "copy" technique and "Rika" shikigami, Tuka Donkae has the ability to essentially stall out a fight indefintely with his jackpot Domain Expansion. As such, we were unable to come to a substantial conclusion for the victor in this fight.

Match 4: Bass da da da vs Jane Juliet

Predicted Winner: Bass da da da

Jane Juliet boasts the largest ever cursed energy output, however, Bass da da da has what is probbaly the most destructive attack in JJK, the ability to summon a black hole. This with her high battle iq and adeptness at close combat fighting gives her the slight edge she needs to win this duel in our opinions.
